Welcome to WARPFIELD's documentation!
======================================

WARPFIELD (\ **W**\inds \ **A**\nd \ **R**\adiation \ **P**\ressure: \ **F**\eedback \ **I**\nduced \ **E**\xpansion, col\ **L**\apse and \ **D**\issolution; `Rahner et al. 2017 <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2017MNRAS.470.4453R/abstract>`_; `Rahner et al. 2019 <https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2019MNRAS.483.2547R/abstract>`_) 
is a python package that facilitates the modeling of HII region evolution and its physical structure.
Our Python package is designed to solve self-consistent equations of motion for a 1D spherical shell, evolving under the influence feedbacks such as stellar winds, injection of supernova energy and radiation pressure, with consideration of gravity.
